## What is a Fantasy Sports App?

A fantasy sports app is an interactive online fantasy sports platform where users create virtual teams made up of real-life athletes and earn points based on those players’ actual performances in live matches.

---

### Key Elements of a Fantasy Sports App

- User Registration & Profiles  
- Team Creation & Player Drafting  
- Real-Time Score Updates  
- Leagues & Competitions  
- Leaderboards & Rankings  
- Prize Pools & Rewards  
- Social Competition
